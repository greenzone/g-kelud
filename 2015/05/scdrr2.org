								<div class="entry-meta">
					<span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://scdrr.org/author/admin/" title="View all posts by admin">admin</a></span> <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://scdrr.org/2016/03/14/online-camporama-meeting/" title="6:21 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">March 14, 2016</span></a>				</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
							</header>
				<div class="entry-content">
				<p class="yiv8979466333h3"><span style="font-family: arial, 'helvetica neue', helvetica, sans-serif;">Tonight we had our Online Meeting for Camporama. If you are going but were unable to attend the meeting, you can listen to it here:</span></p>
<!--[if lt IE 9]><script>document.createElement('audio');</script><![endif]-->
<audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-1806-1" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://scdrr.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Camporama%20Meeting%202016-3-14.mp3?_=1" /><a href="http://scdrr.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Camporama%20Meeting%202016-3-14.mp3">http://scdrr.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Camporama%20Meeting%202016-3-14.mp3</a></audio>
<p>If you have not emailed me your information. Please look at the list below and email me.</p>
<p>1. We need every Outpost to let us know how many are going.</p>
<p>2. Who will be the Point of Contact from each Outpost? Please send a name and email.</p>
<p><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-1807" src="http://scdrr.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/camporama2016-300x264.jpg" alt="camporama2016" width="300" height="264" srcset="http://scdrr.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/camporama2016-300x264.jpg 300w, http://scdrr.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/camporama2016.jpg 350w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" />3. How many hat pins will you need for your Outpost? We recommend 20 pins for every camper at the very least. We will have a cost per pin in the near future.</p>
<p>4. We need full time volunteers for the camp. <a href="http://nationalcamporama.com/volunteers/" target="_blank">Click Here</a> for more information.</p>
<p>5. Are you in FCF and would like to stay and participate in the FCF Village? Go to <a href="http://myroyalrangers.com" target="_blank">myroyalrangers.com</a> and talk to Toby Tarver.</p>
<p>6. Let Randy know if you want to stay at a local church half way to Camporama. He is making contacts and arranging the lodgings.</p>
<p>7. We need volunteers to help make our entryway for the camp, and we need to transport it there and back. This should be a group effort so that no one person is burdened with this responsibility.</p>
<p>Please email me with your information as soon as possible. I look forward to hearing from you.</p>

This will allow him to retain his role in leadership of Royal Rangers USA while we immediately begin a national search for a new director.&#8221;</p>
<p dir="ltr">You can read Mark&#8217;s full letter here: <a href="http://scdrr.org/forms/DougMarsh.pdf" target="_blank">http://scdrr.org/forms/DougMarsh.pdf</a></p>
<p dir="ltr">Please pray that God will guide our National Committee and Leadership in their search for a new director.</p>
